Introduction to Model Selection

Preface

The purpose of this document is to summarise the concepts discussed in the model selection reading club – taking place in the Computational Systems Biology group at the D-BSSE (ETH Zurich) in Winter/Spring 2018.

The reading club is split into two groups:

  • One group covers the introduction into the topic and discusses the commonly used selection criteria Akaike Information Criteria (AIC) and Bayesian Information Criteria (BIC).
  • The second group discusses their extensions for mixed-effects models.

This document summerises the topics of both groups.
